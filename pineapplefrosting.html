Whipped Pineapple Frosting


    Decorates: 15 cupcakes


    Ingredients

  • 4 egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup cane sugar for better results or white sugar
  • 3/4 cup pineapple juice, no pulp
  • 1/2 tsp of cornstarch
  • 1 cup whipping cream
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla

    Directions

  1. Beat egg yolks and sugar until lemon-colored; add pineapple juice and cornstarch, place in saucepan and cook over medium heat until thickened.
  2. Remove from heat and set aside to cool.
  3. Whip cream and vanilla.
  4. Fold 1/3 of whipped cream into mixture, you can do this vigorously.
  5. Fold in the rest of the mixture, it does not have to be thorough, it looks quite nice marbled when piped out.
