Chocolate Cream Pudding Pie Filling


    Servings : 16


    Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 3 1/2 Tbs of cornstarch
  • 3 Tbsp of cocoa powder
  • A little less than â…› tsp of salt
  • 3 cups of whole milk
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1/2 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 1 Tbsp of butter

    Directions

  1. Pour in the sugar, salt, and sieve cornstarch and cocoa powder into a medium saucepan
  2. Beat egg yolks and milk, and pour into the saucepan.
  3. Turn the heat up to high while stirring until the mixture shows signs of boiling, bring the heat down to medium and stir until the mixture thickens.
  4. After mixture thickens, turn off heat and quickly mix in vanilla extract and butter.
  5. Pour into baked pie crust and refrigerate.
  6. If you have extra, pour into custard cup and eat as pudding
  7. Serve out of the fridge with whipped cream for best texture. Enjoy!
