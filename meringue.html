Meringues


    Makes: 10-15


    Ingredients

  • 1 cups sugar
  • 4 eggs whites
  • 1/4 tsp of cream of tartar
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • A dash of salt, about 2 pinches
  • 1/2 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp of rose extract (optional)

    Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 250°F.
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. If you need a template, place it under the parchment paper, you do not need to take them out.
  3. Beat the egg whites until soft peaks form.
  4. Whisk in cream of tartar and salt.
  5. When egg whites start forming firm peaks, start adding in the sugar in quarters.
  6. Once the egg whites have reached stiff glossy peaks add in the vanilla (food coloring as well if desired) and beat for an additional 20 seconds.
  7. Pipe out onto parchment paper
  8. Bake for 1 hour, and then turn off heat. Leave meringues in oven for a minimum of 2 hours or overnight
  9. Searve with strawberries. Enjoy!
