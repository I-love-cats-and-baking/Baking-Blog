Virgin Mojito


    Servings : 1


    Ingredients

  • 15 mint leaves
  • 2 1/2 limes cut into quarter wedges except for 1/2 lime
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp of sugar or until desired taste
  • 1/2 cup of ice cubes
  • 1/2 cup sparkling water

    Directions

  1. Place mint leaves and quartered limes into a cocktail shaker and use a muddler to crush the leaves until you can smell a strong scent of mint.
  2. Add 1 1/2 Tbsp of sugar and continue to muddle and try to get as much lime juice out as possible.
  3. Put in 1/4 cup of the ice, pour the sparkling water over the ice, cap the cocktail maker and shake.
  4. Put in the rest of the ice in a wine glass, and strain the drink into the glass.
  5. Cut the half lime vertically, and put it on the edge of the cup to decorate.
  6. You are now ready to enjoy this wonderful summer drink!
