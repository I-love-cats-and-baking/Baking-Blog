Pineapple Mochi Cake Bars


    Servings : 20


    Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • Pineapple cut into rings, about 1/8 inch
  • 1/4 cup of pinapple juice with pulp (it is easier to just blend a chunk of pinapple)
  • 1 3/4 cups of glutinous rice flour
  • 1/4 cup of cake flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp of baking powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 1/4 cups of sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 cup of coconut milk

    Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F
  2. Butter a 9 by 13 inch cake pan.
  3. Mix 1/4 cup of butter with the brown sugar and pinapple juice.
  4. Pour into cake pan and line bottom of pan with pinapple rings. If you still have space, cut rings in half and line the sides
  5. Sieve the glutinous rice flour, cake flour, baking powder, and salt. Add the sugar and whisk until mixed.
  6. Combine wet ingredients with an electric mixer in a medium mixing bowl, it is easier to combine starting by beating the eggs and vanilla extract first, then adding in the two milks and remaining butter, I recommend using a hand mixer. Avoid overbeating.
  7. Add the wet mixture in three parts, until everything is combined
  8. Pour into cake pan
  9. Bake for one hour, or until a toothpick can come out clean.
  10. Server with a dalop of cherry jam or pineapple frosting and enjoy!
