Pineapple Mochi Cake Bars


    Servings : 20


    Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups of glutinous rice flour
  • 1/4 cup of cake flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp of baking powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 cup of coconut milk

    Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F
  2. Butter a 9 by 13 inch cake pan.
  4. Sieve the glutinous rice flour, cake flour, baking powder, and salt. Add the sugar and whisk until mixed.
  5. Combine wet ingredients with an electric mixer in a medium mixing bowl, it is easier to combine starting by beating the eggs and vanilla extract first, then adding in the two milks, I recommend using a hand mixer. Avoid overbeating.
  6. Add the wet mixture in three parts, until everything is combined
  7. Bake for one hour, or until a toothpick can come out clean.
  8. Drizzle with chocolate ganache and enjoy!
