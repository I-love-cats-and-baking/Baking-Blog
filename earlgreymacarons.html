Earl Grey Macarons


    Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup almond flour
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 Tbs of earl grey tealeaves
  • 2 egg whites
  • 1/4 cup caster sugar
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract

    Directions

  1. Put the almond flour and powdered sugar in a food processor and pulse 3 times. If you pulse too many times the almond oil will cause the powdered sugar to clump.
  2. In a medium mixing bowl beat the egg whites with an electric mixer until soft peaks form. Then gradually pour in the sugar while mixing.
  3. Add the vanilla and continue to beat
  4. The egg whites should now form glossy but stiff peaks. Take about 1/3 of the mixture and fold it in. You can fold the first third quite vigorously, but you should be gentle when folding in the rest.
  5. When the batter is ready it should ribbon off of your spatula. Try to avoid overfolding.
  6. Pipe out macarons onto a template, or 3/4-inch round circles.
