Lemon Chiffon Cake


    Servings : 16


    Ingredients

  • 1 cup of cake flour
  • 3/4 cups white sugar
  • 2 tsp of baking powder
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 3 or 4 eggs, 3 for large sized eggs, seperated
  • 2 Tbsp of milk
  • 1/4 cup room temperature water
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp of lemon extract of 1 Tbs of lemon juice and 1 tsp of zest
  • 1/4 tsp cream of tartar
  • Powdered sugar
  • 2 lemons

    Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F
  2. Sift together, measured flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt into a large mixing bowl. Make a dip in the dry ingredients and put egg yolk, water, milk, vanilla extract, and flavoring in exact order. Set aside.
  3. In a medium mixing bowl, beat egg whites and cream of tartar until stiff.
  4. You can use the same beater to beat flour mix until smooth. Start slowly to avoid flour from flying up and don't overbeat.
  5. Fold in 1/4 of the egg whites which can be done quite roughly as just to loosen the batter. Then gently fold in the rest of the egg whites as to not over fold.
  6. You can now use either a angel food tube pan well cleansed, a 9 inch round or a springform pan lined both on the bottom and sides with parchment for baking.
  7. Once the batter is in the pan lightly tap the pan to release any excess air bubbles
  8. Place a jellyroll pan on the highest rack of your oven, this helps to avoid burning the top of the cake
  9. Let bake for 55 minutes. After 55 minutes, turn up the heat to 350°F and bake for an additional 10 minutes.
  10. Leave your cake to cool in the oven for 10 minutes to avoid shrinkage.
  11. While your cake is still warm transfer it to a plate. Set aside to cool.
  12. Put 1/4 cup of powdered sugar in a small mixing bowl. Add lemon juice and zest(optional) to make into glaze. Keep adding lemon juice until desired consistency, if you add too much, add in extra powdered sugar until desired amount and consistency.
  13. You are now ready to enjoy!
