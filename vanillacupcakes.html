Vanilla Cupcake


    Servings : 16


    Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 3/4 cups sugar
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup of milk, or for better results a 1/2 cup of whipping cream and 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1 Tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of water

    Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F and line cupcake liners
  2. Sift flour and cornstarch 2-3 times, then sift in the sugar, baking powder, and salt into a large mixing bowl.
  3. Create a well and add the oil, eggs, milk, and vanilla extract in this exact order. Beat until smooth, but donâ€™t overbeat.
  4. Slowly add in the water by hand whisking.
  5. The batter should be very liquid like.
  6. Fill the cupcakes about halfway and bake for 15 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean when you poke it.
  7. While cupcakes are still slightly warm, remove cupcakes to cool on either a cooling rack or a plate.
  8. Decorate and enjoy!
